I — Why Liminal Light Exists

Space to
transition intentionally.

Modern life asks us to move constantly — from work to family, from responsibilities to endless notifications. Rarely are we given space to transition intentionally.

Liminal Light exists to help people move intentionally between the demands of life and the restoration they need to thrive.

Every treatment is designed as more than a massage. It's an opportunity to slow your nervous system, reconnect with your body, and leave feeling more grounded than when you arrived.

Mobile brings the practice to you — no commute, no gap between work and your session, no need to leave a space you already feel at ease in. It's built for convenience: the treatment folds into your day exactly where you already are.

Home Studio offers the opposite gift: distance. A private, dedicated room in St James, set apart from your inbox, your kitchen, your to-do list — for the days when a change of scene is as restorative as the massage itself.
