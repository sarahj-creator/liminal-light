before you lie down.

The corridor opens around you in limestone and amber. Above, a net installation suspends in the ceiling — catching the light, dispersing it, so the glow itself breathes.

II — The Philosophy
"Liminal" means threshold. The space between one state and another.

Most wellness spaces ask you to relax once you're on the table. Liminal Light begins the work the moment you cross the door.

When we say sanctuary, we do not mean a room with candles and calm music. We mean a space designed to dismantle the accumulated weight of the ordinary world.
